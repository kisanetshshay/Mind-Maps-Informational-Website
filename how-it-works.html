How It Works

Without structured training, many farmers
struggle with low productivity, financial
insecurity, and efficient cooperative
engagement.

Grouping Farmers

Farmers are grouped per
village to groups.

Assigning Extensions

Extension workers are assigned to
each group of farmersin the villages.

Scheduling Sessions

Cooerative admin shedules training
sessions to each of the farmer's
groups at least twice a month.

Farmer's Points

Farmer's attendance rate
converts to reward points.

Training Session Cost

Each training session costs
an amount that a farmer must
pay in order to attend.
